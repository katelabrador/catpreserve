South Florida Wildcat Prserve

A bit about us!

Located in Arcadia, FL, the South Florida Wildcat preserve helps rescue and rehabilitate neglected wildcats that would not survive in the wild due to domestication. All the research data collected here helps other organizations world wide to protect these beautiful, and endangered, cats.
